Personal Weather Station Self-Registration

For Personal Weather Station registration:
  - Select the correspondent Weather Station Model and Type below.
  - Your station will be registered on Weather Underground and a new unique PWS ID will be issued to your device.

Your PWS and weather data will be shown on:
  - The Blue Horizon Device Map
  - The Weather Underground's Personal Weather Station Network
Weather data will be posted for public viewing.
